# UrbanPulse AI

## 🌆 Reimagining Future Cities Through Accessibility, Affordability, and AI

UrbanPulse AI is an intelligent urban analytics platform designed to help citizens, urban planners, researchers, and policymakers evaluate neighborhoods through the lens of accessibility, sustainability, and affordability.

Inspired by the **15-Minute City** concept, UrbanPulse AI combines real-world location data, interactive mapping, urban accessibility analysis, artificial intelligence, and financial sustainability insights to create a comprehensive future-city planning experience.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

As cities continue to grow, residents often struggle with:

* Long commuting times
* Unequal access to essential services
* Traffic congestion
* Poor urban planning decisions
* Lack of green spaces
* Limited healthcare accessibility
* High living costs
* Difficulty evaluating neighborhood quality

Most existing solutions provide maps or demographic information but fail to offer actionable insights that connect accessibility, future planning, and affordability.

UrbanPulse AI aims to bridge that gap.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

UrbanPulse AI helps users answer three important questions:

### 1. How accessible is my neighborhood?

Analyze access to:

* Healthcare facilities
* Schools
* Grocery stores
* Parks
* Public transportation
* Essential daily services

### 2. How can my neighborhood improve?

Generate AI-powered recommendations and future planning strategies to improve accessibility and sustainability.

### 3. Is my neighborhood financially sustainable?

Evaluate living costs, affordability, savings potential, and financial well-being for families.

## 🏗️ How It Works

### Step 1

User enters a location.

### Step 2

The platform gathers geographic and amenity data.

### Step 3

Accessibility metrics are calculated.

### Step 4

A 15-Minute Accessibility Score is generated.

### Step 5

Interactive maps and analytics are displayed.

### Step 6

AI analyzes accessibility gaps.

### Step 7

Future planning recommendations are generated.

### Step 8

Smart Living Advisor evaluates affordability and financial sustainability.
